Why this matters

Calibration transforms good discrimination (85.5%) into exceptional (95.2%). ECE reduction means confidence scores actually mean something.

Benchmarked on GPT-2-large. KAIROS = weakly-supervised assessor + isotonic calibration + coverage control.

Result: 89.1% selective quality at 28.1% coverage (ECE 3.4%, AUC 95.2%).
